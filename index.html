BoardPass+

boardpassplus.com ↗

BoardPass+ is a full-stack exam prep platform built for financial professionals sitting for the CFP®, SIE, Series 7, and Series 66 board exams. We designed and developed the entire product from concept to launch — custom Next.js frontend, Firebase authentication, Firestore database, and Stripe billing.

The AI integration is what makes it different. Every session is powered by an intelligent coaching engine — analyzing wrong answers, identifying mistake patterns, and building a personalized study plan for the following day. It doesn't just grade you. It learns how you think.

Inside the Product

How BoardPass+ actually works

BoardPass+ isn't just a question bank. It's a fully integrated study system that adapts to how you learn, where you struggle, and what you need to work on next — automatically.

01
Weighted Practice Questions
4,000+ questions across CFP®, SIE, Series 7, and Series 66 — weighted to match real exam difficulty. Every answer includes a brain-emoji memory cue designed to make the correct answer stick long-term.
02
AI-Powered Coach
After each session, the AI-powered coach analyzes your wrong answers, identifies the pattern in your mistakes, and builds a targeted study plan for tomorrow. It doesn't just tell you what you got wrong — it figures out why, and what to do next.
03
Progress Tracking & History
Every session is stored. You can track accuracy by domain, review past sessions, and see your improvement over time. The platform gets smarter the more you use it.
